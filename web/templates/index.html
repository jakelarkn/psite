Summary Who am I? What am I doing?

Hi. I'm a Graduate Student in Computer Science and Engineering. I grew up in New York City but I've been in the Bay Area now for the better part of a decade. I completed my undergrad at Stanford Univeristy with a B.S. in Mathematics and Computational Science. I am passionate about C.S., applied math and techology and I am excited to work in world that's becoming increasingly data-driven. Currently, I am nearing completion of a Masters degree at in Computer Science and Engineering at Santa Clara University and I live in San Francisco.

My academic interests span a broad range including Distributed Systems, Scalabity, Data Mining, Parallel Processing, Web Development. I started programming in C/C++. My recent work has been primarily in Server-side (backend) programming in Python, and work on the web front-end in Javascript, and HTML/CSS. I'm currently experimenting with Objective-C and iOS.

Outside of work, I enjoy Running, Cycling, Hiking, Mountaineering, Skiing and Photography.

This site is under development. I will be making it more dynamic over time.
